1 Morning everyone



Two days ago, Aasish Sir shared me a message:

March 30, 2017 (Friday)

For people graduating in 2019 and 2020 if you want to apply in Microsoft for FTE or intern, send your resume to sanjliagarwal@gmail.com.

2 Initial thoughts

What an opportunity! And here I’m, not even have prepared my Resume till now…

2.1 Why?

Um-mm, I had planned to prepare it in summers just like earlier when Dev and Abhijit made their own. Ample time to think “What goes in and What not“.

2.2 What I missed?

Precious time, to manage and prepare better in advance. On top of that, today was the last the date and I’d to go to the College for some paperwork and registrations.

3 A weird idea

Due to the hurry for which only I was responsible, I couldn’t think of any experiments with the layout/structure and contents of the resume.

Was just… total silence!

Later a wierd thought came to use Dev’s Resume as a template, in the time of peril.

4 The discussion

In the meantime, Sir suggested me to include a cover letter and try to highlight my strengths and personality.

For once I thought that whether Sir meant by the usual formal-format resume rather than the powerpoint one which I was thinking.

So He clarified that I should I continue and try not to use too many colours and also to take care of consistency throughout the presentation.

4.1 But hey…

Till now Sir didn’t knew that I was about to use a template rather than experimenting out myself.

This was soon going to be a mistake!

And I continued…

5 The result

Though was not at all satisfied because all the work had lost it’s authenticity. Still happy with whateverI could do in the limited time.



Even though I used a template, I tried to think/use my own beliefs and personality traits as accurate and authentic as I could given the time I’d left.

Shared with the Sir (had mailed it to the Ma’am too) and He replied:+1:

5.1 Words to cherrish

  • Please note that templates are the barriers for creativity.
  • Be inspired , but stay open to experiment with different forms.
  • Idea is to communicate the key messages about you effectively
  • In a manner that is impactful.
  • Right messages are drawn.

And the receiver should have a smile on face the moment your profile comes across!

6 Moments later

Suddenly Sir shared a convo:



Sir had shared the resume with a different group and then replied:

In my eyes resume is good.

However u may want to review from a point of someone unknown.
It will be understood by people who respect independent thinking.

Beautiful words once again! Here’s how I replied back:

😋 Honest feedback is little hard these days… Really happy to hear that. 🙏🙏 For it is the opportunity ✨ to improve even further!

We are only as good as the exposure, ✌

I'll definitely devote decent 🕐 time in thought process from the audience perspective first before doing anything.

Will work from scratch, Keeping those points in mind.

Thank you Sir, for sharing, 🙏

I was fortunate enough to get such a wonderful opportunity to learn and improve further!

7 TODO Current plan

Will do a fair amount of experimentation first, gather feddback and improvise. Then see how it goes.
